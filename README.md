## ğŸ’¡ Concept: Turn Boring Meetings into a Game

**Category:** Business / Technology

**Meeting Bingo Generator** is an interactive web application that gamifies corporate meetings. By inputting a meeting agenda, industry, and participant roles, **Gemini 3 Pro** generates a custom 5x5 Bingo card filled with phrases likely to be spoken during that specific context.

It transforms the passive endurance of long meetings into an active, humorous engagement, leveraging AI to predict human behavior in corporate settings.

## ğŸš€ Key Features

### 1. Context-Aware Phrase Generation (Powered by Gemini 3 Pro)
*   **Input:** Meeting Topic (e.g., "Q4 Budget Review"), Industry, Participant Roles.
*   **Output:** 25 contextually relevant "clichÃ©" phrases generated by Gemini 3 Pro.
*   **Role-Based Precision:** Selecting specific roles (e.g., "Sales Manager", "CTO") allows Gemini to predict role-specific jargon, increasing the accuracy and humor of the bingo card.

### 2. Interactive Gameplay
*   **Live Bingo Board:** Tap tiles as phrases are spoken.
*   **Win Detection:** Automatic detection of Bingo (lines, diagonal).
*   **Timer & Leaderboard:** Records the time to Bingo to rank the "predictability" of meetings.

### 3. AI "Boringness" Analysis
*   Gemini 3 Pro analyzes the completed Bingo pattern and the speed of completion to generate a witty commentary on how boring or stereotypical the meeting was.

### 4. Social & Global
*   **Share Results:** Easily share your Bingo victory on X (Twitter), Facebook, and Instagram.
*   **Multi-language Support:** Works in English, Japanese, German, French, and other major languages.

This project leverages the specific strengths of **Gemini 3 Pro**:

*   **Deep Contextual Understanding:** Unlike simple random word generators, Gemini 3 Pro understands the subtle nuances of different industries and corporate cultures to generate phrases that are funny because they are *true*.
*   **Vibe Coding:** The core logic and UI components were rapidly prototyped and refined using Gemini's coding capabilities in AI Studio, demonstrating the power of natural language programming.
*   **Dynamic Analysis:** The app uses the model not just for generation, but for analyzing the *result* of the game to provide feedback.
